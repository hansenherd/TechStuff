c:forEach is useful to iterate over data.

<c:forEach var="thing" items="${things}" varStatus="status">

    <a href="${thing.link}">${thing.description}</a>

    ${not status.last ? '<hr/>' : ''}

</c:forEach>

The important bits of the above snippet are the varStatus="status" and status.last. 
The first one defines a 'status' variable, and the second one accesses it to ask if 
this row is the 'last'.

The status object has a number of useful methods:

current, index, count, first, last, begin, step, end
